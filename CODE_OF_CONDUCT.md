# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want makershelf to be a welcoming and constructive project for everyone.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful
- Be helpful
- Assume good intent
- Give actionable feedback
- Focus on the work, not the person

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment or discrimination
- Personal attacks
- Trolling or repeated disruption
- Publishing private information without consent

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior harms the community.
